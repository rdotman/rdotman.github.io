The Role of Keywords in SEO

Keywords are the terms and phrases that users input into search engines like Google. They are crucial for SEO as they indicate what users are actively looking for.

Steps for Conducting Keyword Research

  1. List down terms that are relevant to your business.
  2. Utilize a keyword analysis tool to gather data on these terms.
  3. Initially, opt for keywords with lower search volumes and difficulty levels.

Examining Competitors on SERP

Studying your competitors on the Search Engine Results Page (SERP) can offer additional keyword ideas and insights into the content your audience prefers.
