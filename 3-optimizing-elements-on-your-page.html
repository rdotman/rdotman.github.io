Fine-Tuning On-Page Components

Title Tags

Title tags are crucial elements that signify the content of a webpage. They are prominently displayed in search results.

Meta Descriptions

Meta descriptions offer a brief summary of what each webpage is about, providing valuable information to both Google and your audience.

Header Tags

Header tags help to organize your content in a hierarchical manner. The main title should be an H1 tag, followed by subheadings as H2 to H6 tags.
